My Magical Star House

Hello! I’m Tom, and I live in an amazing house shaped like a giant star! It’s in a small village with green hills around it. Our star house is big, shiny, and very special. It has three floors and a twinkly[1] garden in front of it. In the garden, there are bright flowers, a small fountain, and a glowing[2] star statue. A silver fence goes around the garden, and it sparkles[3] in the sunlight.

On the ground floor, there is a cosy living room, a shiny kitchen, and a bathroom. The living room is my favourite place. There is a big, soft sofa, a television, and a huge star-shaped window. We watch films and play board games there. The kitchen is silver and bright. My mum cooks tasty meals there, and we eat at a star-shaped table. In the bathroom, there is a round bathtub and a shiny mirror.

On the first floor, there are three bedrooms and another bathroom. My bedroom is small but magical. There is a blue bed, a desk for my homework, and a shelf with my favourite books and toys. My sister’s bedroom is purple, and there is a small telescope by her window to look at the stars. My parents’ bedroom is the biggest. There is a large bed and a wardrobe for their clothes.

On the top floor, there is a special mysterious room. There is a big telescope and comfortable chairs. We look at the night sky and see real stars! Outside, there is a garage for our car. In the backyard, there is a swing shaped like a crescent moon[4] and a table for picnics under the stars.

Our house is full of magic and light. Every room feels warm and happy. I love my star house!
